Project Overview

This proposal outlines "Reactor Rush," a high-impact VR experience designed to attract, engage, and immerse potential recruits. It translates the high-stakes, mission-critical environment of HMNB Clyde into a fast-paced, 90-second game that gamifies engineering problem-solving.

Primary Objective

To create a memorable, short-form interactive experience that converts casual interest into high-quality recruitment leads by directly showcasing the challenging and essential nature of engineering careers within the fleet.

The 90-Second Journey

The user's experience is a carefully orchestrated 4-phase flow, moving from a cinematic hook to a high-pressure incident, through the core gameplay loop, and concluding with a satisfying resolution. The vast majority of time is dedicated to the interactive gameplay itself.

Visual & Experience Flow

  1. 1

    Cinematic Opening (5-8s)

    A stylized submarine glides underwater, then a rapid zoom transitions to FPV in the engineering bay.

  2. 2

    Immersive Incident (3-5s)

    Red lights flash. Sirens blare. The Captain shouts the order: "Get that reactor under control!"

  3. 3

    Core Gameplay (80-90s)

    The fast-paced, color-coded task system begins. The user must react, grab tools, and stabilize the reactor.

  4. 4

    Session Conclusion (3s)

    Reactor stabilizes. Lights normalize. The Captain offers praise: "Excellent work, engineer!"

Interactive Gameplay Concept

The core mechanic is a simple, high-speed task-matching system. The reactor signals a problem (color), and the player must select the correct tool and perform the right action. This demo simulates the *decision-making* part of that loop.

Scoring System

  • Correct Action: +100 points
  • Speed Bonus: +20 to +50 points
  • Combo Streaks: Multiplier increases (x1.2, x1.5, x2)
  • Mistakes: 0 points or small time penalty

Game Feel

  • Fast, punchy, and satisfying haptics
  • Clear, distinct audio cues for success
  • Big, bold visual feedback from the reactor
  • High-pressure, but fun—not stressful

Engagement & Recruitment Value

This tool is not just a game; it's a high-ROI asset designed specifically to meet recruitment goals by creating a memorable and shareable experience that directly links to the core skills of an engineer.

Short & Replayable

Maximizes event throughput, minimizes queue frustration, and encourages re-tries for high scores.
